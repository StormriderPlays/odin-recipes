Cinnamon Apple Juice Whiskey

A delightfully scrumptious drink that feels like its giving you a warm hug

Ingredients

  • Fireball Cinnamon Whiskey
  • Any Non-Alcoholic Apple Juice or Cider

Preparation Steps

  1. Gather the Fireball and Apple Juice together along with a cup (An 10-12 ounce cup is recommended).

    2. Normal Strength

  2. Pour 2 ounces (1 part) Fireball and 8 ounces (4 parts) Apple Juice.

    3. Higher Strength (Skip Step 2)

  3. Pour 2 ounces (1 part) Fireball and 4 ounces (2 parts) Apple Juice.
  4. The drink is mixed as it's poured. Add ice if you wish to chill your drink. Enjoy!
